FAQs

Are the products sold online new?

Yes, we assure you that all the products are brand new.

How many for wholesale?

For wholesale 20PCS & UP and 3PCS per item. While for retail 20PCS Below.

Can I refund my money or replace wrong item and color?

Yes, we can refund your money. Or we replace the wrong item and color. Just make sure that you provide video proof of unboxing the parcel. You can send the video through the Contact Us.

How long will the delivery take?

The website receives orders 24/7 but our store pick up has a cut off time from Wednesday through Fridays only. All orders placed before the cut off time will be processed and picked up from our store on the same day. Delivery within Metro Manila shall take one (1) to three (3) business days. Provincial area deliveries will have duration of five (5) to seven (7) business days. All periods shall commence from the day of pick-up from our store. Please note that we aim to deliver within the time agreed upon at purchase. However, we do not guarantee that all orders will be delivered within the presented time frame.

How can I pay for the item(s) I purchased??

The available payment as of now are Cash on Delivery, Cash on Pick up and Shopee checkout.
